Agencies, Acts & Schemes

  • 106th Constitutional Amendment Act (2023): Known as Nari Shakti Vandan Adhiniyam. Mandates 33% reservation for women (incl. SC/ST) in Lok Sabha and State Assemblies. Validity: 15 years. Relevant Articles: 239AA (Delhi), 330A (Lok Sabha), 332A (State Assembly), 334A (Sunset Clause).
  • Article 79: Defines the Parliament of India as consisting of the President, Lok Sabha, and Rajya Sabha. The Hindi names were adopted in 1954.
  • India Semiconductor Mission (ISM): Launched in 2021 to build a vibrant semiconductor and display ecosystem.
  • PM E-DRIVE Scheme: Launched in October 2024 to accelerate EV adoption and build electric vehicle charging infrastructure.

The Mains Analytical Breakdown

Dimensional Analysis

Social Impact
  • The 106th CAA (Women's Reservation Bill) guarantees a critical mass of female representation (33%) in policy-making, radically altering the patriarchal structure of Indian legislatures.
Economic Impact
  • The India Semiconductor Mission (2021) and the setup of plants in Sanand (Gujarat), Sulur, and Palladam (TN) will drastically reduce India's import dependency on critical tech components, fostering a robust electronics ecosystem.
Political Impact
  • The official transition of Andhra Pradesh's capital to Amaravati, following the 10-year stipulation in the AP Reorganization Act 2014, underscores the complex administrative friction of state bifurcation.
Environmental Impact
  • The PM E-DRIVE Scheme (launched Oct 2024) drives infrastructure growth for EV charging, mitigating fossil-fuel reliance amid rising global fuel prices.
Pros & Cons Analysis

Advantages

  • EV Push (PM E-DRIVE): Insulates the Indian economy from volatile international oil prices and limits carbon footprints in urban centers.
  • Semiconductor Independence: Domestic chips like ISRO's Vikram 3201 ensure national security in strategic defense and space sectors.

Challenges

  • Women's Reservation Bottlenecks: Implementation relies on delimitation and census completion, potentially delaying actual electoral parity.
  • EV Infrastructure Lag: Rapid commercial EV rollout is hindered by grid capability and the lack of high-density charging stations in older residential clusters.

Relevant Committee Reports & Frameworks

  • India Semiconductor Mission (ISM) 2021: The nodal framework under Digital India Corporation guiding the establishment of semiconductor fabs and testing centers like those in Sanand and Tamil Nadu.

  • Article 79 defines the Parliament as consisting of the President, the Lok Sabha, and the Rajya Sabha.

  • The Vice President of India is considered a constituent part of the Parliament under Article 79.
  • A1 and 2 only

  • C1 and 3 only
  • D1, 2, and 3
Statement 1: Correct ✓
Statement 2: Correct ✓
Statement 3: Incorrect ✗
✔ Answer: A

Statement 3 is incorrect. Article 79 only includes the President and the two Houses (Lok Sabha and Rajya Sabha) as parts of the Parliament. The Vice President is the ex-officio Chairman of the Rajya Sabha but is not listed as a distinct component of Parliament in Article 79.
