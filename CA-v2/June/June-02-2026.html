Agencies, Acts & Schemes

  • Maha Water Mission: Launched jointly by the Anusandhan National Research Foundation and the Ministry of Jal Shakti. Allocated budget: ₹200 Crores. Focuses on promoting startups and deep tech in the water sector.
  • Jal Jeevan Mission: Launched on Aug 15, 2019. Provides 55 litres per person per day to rural households. The target has been extended from 2024 to 2028. Mission Director: Kamal Kishore Soan. Minister: CR Patil.
  • Swarna Jayanti Shahari Rozgar Yojana (SJSRY): Launched on Dec 1, 1997, by the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs to address urban unemployment via skill development and self-employment.

⬡ Tamil Nadu Connect

  • Judicial Milestone: The direct elevation of V. Mohana to the Supreme Court from a Senior Advocate position marks a significant judicial achievement for legal professionals hailing from Tamil Nadu.
  • Literacy Status Context: Re-emphasized the educational parameters distinguishing Fully Literate states like Sikkim against demographic challenges in highly populated states like TN and Kerala's unique literacy metrics.

The Mains Analytical Breakdown

Dimensional Analysis

Social Impact
  • Schemes like SJSRY directly address the growing urban unemployment crisis by providing critical skill development and self-employment avenues to the youth.
Economic Impact
  • The CEPA with Oman structurally integrates Indian products into Middle-Eastern markets by drastically reducing tariffs, thereby boosting domestic export viability.
Political Impact
  • The political elevation of Kalita Majhi in West Bengal showcases the potential for profound grassroots political empowerment, transcending traditional socio-economic barriers.
Environmental Impact
  • The implementation of the Skycast System at Delhi Airport utilizes sonar/radar data to mitigate climate-induced aviation risks, ensuring safer operations during extreme weather.
Pros & Cons Analysis

Advantages

  • Trade Expansion (CEPA): Free Trade Agreements lower costs for consumers on imported goods and open lucrative international markets for domestic manufacturers.
  • Water Innovation: The Maha Water Mission injects ₹200 Crores into the startup ecosystem, specifically encouraging deep-tech solutions for water conservation and purification.

Challenges

  • Domestic Market Threats: Unrestricted Free Trade Agreements can expose domestic MSMEs to stiff competition from cheaper, mass-produced foreign imports.
  • Export Quality Control: Japan's ban on Indian mangoes highlights the severe economic consequences of failing to adhere to stringent international pest control and agricultural standards.

Relevant Committee Reports & Frameworks

  • Mansfield Committee (1886): Appointed by Lord Dufferin, this historical commission was tasked with studying and restructuring the Indian Currency System.
  • Sri Krishna Committee (2010): Played a pivotal role in examining the demands for a separate state, leading to the formation of Telangana in 2014 under the AP Reorganization Act.

Math Question: 12% of 250 L is the same as x% of 150 L. Find the value of x.
  • A15
  • B18
  • C20
  • D25
✔ Answer: C

12% of 250 = (12/100) * 250 = 30. Then, x% of 150 = 30. Therefore, (x/100) * 150 = 30 => 1.5x = 30 => x = 20.
