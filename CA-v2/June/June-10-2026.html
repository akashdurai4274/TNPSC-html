The Fact-Sheet (Prelims Focus)

Category Extracted Data Points & Details
TN Admin & Schemes Singappen Special Task Force: Launched on June 9, 2026, at Rajarathinam Stadium, Chennai. Focus: Women & Children safety in public spaces (schools/colleges/bus stands).
Budget & Scale: ₹354.66 Crores. Creation of 2545 new posts. Comprises 270 units across TN.

Agencies, Acts & Schemes

  • Swadesh Darshan Scheme: Initiated by the Ministry of Tourism.
    – 1.0 (2014-15): Adopted a Theme-based tourist circuit approach (e.g., Jyotirlinga circuit).
    – 2.0 (2023): Pivoted to a Destination-based approach for holistic development.
  • IIPS: International Institute of Population Sciences. Co-publisher of the NFHS reports along with the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare.

⬡ Tamil Nadu Connect: Evolution of Women in Police

  • 1973: Women were inducted into the Tamil Nadu Police force for the first time during the tenure of Kalaignar Karunanidhi.
  • 1992: Tamil Nadu's first All Women Police Station (AWPS) established in Thousand Lights, Chennai (during Jayalalithaa's tenure). Note: India's first AWPS is in Kozhikode, Kerala.
  • 2003: First Women Commando Unit established.
  • 2019 & Later: Amma Patrol introduced by EPS; later rebranded/updated as Pink Patrol by M.K. Stalin. Pink Squad deployed in Chennai Metro.

The Mains Analytical Breakdown

Dimensional Analysis

Social Impact
  • The deployment of the Singappen Task Force creates a visible deterrent in high-risk public zones, fostering psychological security for female students and working women.
Economic Impact
  • A staggering drop in the TFR to 0.9 in Andaman foreshadows a severe future contraction of the working-age population, threatening the islands' long-term economic sustainability.
Political Impact
  • Naming 21 unnamed Andaman islands after Param Vir Chakra awardees is a strategic soft-power move to integrate remote territories with mainland nationalistic sentiment.
Agrarian / Environmental Impact
  • Facilitating international export corridors for GI-tagged crops like the Tezpur Litchi elevates the global agrarian profile of Northeast India, reducing regional dependency.
Pros & Cons Analysis

Pros: Specialized Task Forces (e.g., Singappen)

  • Focused Mandate: Unlike regular law enforcement which is overburdened with general duties, this force is exclusively trained for rapid response to gender-based violence.
  • Employment Boost: Generating 2,545 new vacancies directly enhances women's representation within the state machinery.

Challenges: Specialized Task Forces

  • Domestic vs. Public Crime: Statistics indicate women face higher rates of domestic violence (behind closed doors) than public harassment, which public patrolling cannot efficiently prevent.
  • Fiscal Strain: Setting up new uniforms, specialized vehicles, and training (budgeted at ₹354.66 Cr) creates recurring financial overhead for the state exchequer.

Relevant Committee Reports & Frameworks

  • Lyall Commission (1897): Appointed by Viceroy Lord Elgin II to investigate the severe Indian famine of 1896-1897 and recommend administrative mitigation measures.
