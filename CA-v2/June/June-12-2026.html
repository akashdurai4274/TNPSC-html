Category Extracted Data Points & Details

The Mains Analytical Breakdown

Dimensional Analysis

Social Impact
  • The Supreme Court formally evaluating a homemaker's notional monthly income at ₹30,000 provides structural recognition to women's invisible, unpaid domestic labor, a milestone for gender justice.
Economic Impact
  • Tamil Nadu's revised target of a 1.5 Trillion Dollar economy by 2036 underscores an aggressive pivot towards advanced manufacturing and service sector expansion to maintain its leading GDP contributor status.
Political Impact
  • The 11th NITI Aayog Governing Council meeting highlighted the complexities of 'Cooperative Federalism', where states actively negotiate fiscal autonomy against Centrally Sponsored Schemes' conditionalities (e.g., NEP implementation).
Ecological / Health Impact
  • The repeated emergence of the zoonotic Nipah virus in Kerala stresses the urgency of developing robust local epidemiological surveillance systems to mitigate recurring public health emergencies.
Pros & Cons Analysis: High-Altitude Infra

Pros: Projects like Zojila Tunnel

  • Strategic Mobility: Ensures all-weather connectivity between J&K and Ladakh, critical for rapid troop deployment in border regions.
  • Economic Integration: Eliminates the winter lockdown of local economies, enabling uninterrupted trade and tourism flow.

Cons: Projects like Zojila Tunnel

  • Ecological Disruption: Heavy construction and blasting in the fragile Himalayan ecosystem exacerbate the risk of landslides and glacial retreats.
  • Fiscal Burden: Building and maintaining a 13km high-altitude single-tube tunnel demands exorbitant capital expenditure.

Relevant Committee Reports & Frameworks

  • Butler Commission (1927): Appointed under Lord Irwin. Investigated and sought to clarify the paramountcy relationship between the British Crown and the Indian Princely States. (Often studied as a historical precursor to modern center-state relations).
