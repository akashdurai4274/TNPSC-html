The Mains Analytical Breakdown

Dimensional Analysis

Internal Security Impact
  • The launch of Operation Chakra-VI by the CBI highlights the evolving nature of cybercrimes (like "Digital Arrest" frauds) and the necessity for federal agencies to adopt sophisticated tech countermeasures.
Economic & Defence Impact
  • Elevating GRSE to a Navratna CPSE empowers India's indigenous shipbuilding sector with greater financial autonomy, enhancing rapid procurement and production of naval assets.
Social Justice & Polity Impact
  • Revisiting the Mandal Commission (1979) underscores the historical struggle for equity. Identifying 3743 castes for 27% reservation fundamentally altered the socio-political landscape of modern India.
Environmental Diplomacy
  • The expansion of the International Big Cat Alliance (IBCA) with Bangladesh joining solidifies India's soft-power leadership in global ecological conservation efforts across the sub-continent.
Pros & Cons Analysis: CBI's Cyber Operations (Chakra-VI)

Pros: Advanced Threat Mitigation

  • Targeted Crackdown: Directly dismantles highly organized transnational cyber-fraud syndicates that utilize fear tactics (Digital Arrests) to extort citizens.
  • Tech-Driven Governance: The integration of tools like the 'ABHAI' AI Chatbot enhances the agency's ability to process massive datasets and track illicit financial flows quickly.

Cons: Structural & Jurisdictional Hurdles

  • Statutory Ambiguity: Because the CBI is not a statutory body (relying on the archaic DSPE Act, 1946), state-level general consent issues frequently stall pan-India operations.
  • Evolving Anonymity: Cyber criminals constantly adapt using dark web networks and cryptocurrencies, often outpacing the technological upgrade cycle of federal agencies.

Relevant Committee Reports & Frameworks

  • Santhanam Committee (1962-1964): The Committee on Prevention of Corruption was appointed under K. Santhanam. It recommended the establishment of the Central Vigilance Commission (CVC) and heavily influenced the formation of the CBI in 1963 to systematically combat administrative corruption.
