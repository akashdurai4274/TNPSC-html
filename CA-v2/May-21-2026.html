Global Organizations & Acts

  • FAO (Food and Agriculture Organization): Established on October 16, 1945 (World Food Day). Headquartered in Rome, Italy. The Agricola Medal is given for promoting global food security and poverty eradication.
  • UIDAI: Unique Identification Authority of India. Statutory authority mandated to issue Aadhaar, under the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology.
  • Indo-Sri Lanka Accord (1987): Bilateral agreement resulting in the deployment of the Indian Peace Keeping Force (IPKF) to Sri Lanka.

⬡ Tamil Nadu Connect

The Mains Analytical Breakdown

Dimensional Analysis

Social Impact
  • The return of the Chola Copper Plates from Leiden University corrects a historical cultural void, reviving tangible links to Tamil antiquity and the inclusive religious policies (Buddhist Vihara grants) of the Chola dynasty.
Economic Impact
  • The appointment of a new UIDAI CEO highlights the ongoing consolidation of India's digital public infrastructure, crucial for eliminating leakages in direct benefit transfers and welfare schemes.
Political Impact
  • The expansion of the Tamil Nadu cabinet to include Congress after nearly six decades is a strategic maneuver demonstrating the mechanics of coalition politics and proportional representation in regional governance.
  • The observance of Anti-Terrorism Day (May 21) serves as a stark reminder of the geopolitical complexities and domestic repercussions stemming from the 1987 Indo-Sri Lanka Accord.
Environmental Impact
  • Recognitions like the UNESCO World Heritage status for the Sundarbans (West Bengal) highlight global mandates to protect fragile coastal mangroves vital for biodiversity and cyclone mitigation.
Pros & Cons Analysis

Advantages

  • Space Diplomacy (Shukrayaan): Sweden's inclusion in India's Venus mission reduces financial burdens, facilitates technology transfer, and elevates ISRO's status as a global hub for cost-effective planetary exploration.
  • Soft Power: High-profile awards (FAO, Sweden, Norway) and diplomatic gestures (Year of Culture 2027) heavily bolster India's geopolitical leverage.

Challenges

  • Regional Geopolitics: Historical interventions (like IPKF in Sri Lanka) leave lingering trust deficits, complicating current diplomatic and electoral narratives within Tamil Nadu regarding Sri Lankan Tamil welfare.

Relevant Committee Reports & Frameworks

  • Cultural Exchange Treaties: The mechanism through which artifacts like the Leiden University Chola plates are repatriated involves high-level diplomatic frameworks prioritizing the return of post-colonial heritage.
