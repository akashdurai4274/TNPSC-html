The Mains Analytical Breakdown

Dimensional Analysis

Social Impact
  • Schemes like SMILE directly address the structural inequalities faced by marginalized groups such as transgender individuals and destitute beggars, aiming for complete rehabilitation rather than punitive measures.
Economic Impact
  • Bilateral funding, such as Japan's support for the Tamil Nadu Urban Health Care Project, bridges infrastructural financing gaps, allowing states to elevate tertiary care capabilities without exhausting fiscal reserves.
Political Impact
  • The meticulous structuring of Parliamentary Committees (PAC, Estimates, CoPU) and the customary appointment of an Opposition leader to head the PAC ensures robust executive accountability and financial prudence.
Environmental Impact
  • The International Big Cat Alliance (IBCA), headquartered in New Delhi, positions India as a global leader in mega-fauna conservation, expanding the successful template of Project Tiger to a multi-national scale.
Pros & Cons Analysis

Advantages

  • Global Leadership: Establishing the IBCA headquarters in New Delhi enhances India's soft power and environmental diplomacy.
  • Legislative Scrutiny: Dedicated financial committees (Estimates, PAC) with proportional representation from all parties effectively check public expenditure.

Challenges

  • Conservation Complexities: While protecting 7 big cats is noble, introducing species like the Cheetah requires navigating complex biological and climatic adaptations.
  • Internal Security: Events like the Baloch Liberation Army's attack on a Pakistani train serve as stark reminders of cross-border and regional militancy threatening South Asian stability.

Relevant Committee Reports & Frameworks

  • Financial Committees of Parliament:
    1. Public Accounts Committee (PAC): Examines CAG reports. Headed by Opposition (K.C. Venugopal).
    2. Estimates Committee: Largest committee (30 members, all LS). Suggests alternative policies for economy. Headed by Sanjay Jaiswal.
    3. Committee on Public Undertakings (CoPU): Examines reports of public sector entities. Headed by Baijayant Panda.
    Note: Ministers are barred from membership in these committees.
