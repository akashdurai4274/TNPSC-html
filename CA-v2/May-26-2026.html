The Fact-Sheet (Prelims Focus)

Category Extracted Data Points & Details
Art, History & Culture Kumbhalgarh Fort: Located in Rajasthan (Aravalli Range). Built by Rajput King Rana Kumbha (Mewar Province) in the 15th Century. It features the second longest continuous wall globally (36 km). Note: Great Wall of China is the longest (~21,000 km). Conferred UNESCO World Heritage status in 2013.
Parliamentary Facts New Parliament Building: Named "Sansad Bhavan". Lok Sabha has 888 seats (Peacock theme). Rajya Sabha has 384 seats (Lotus theme).
Old Parliament: Renamed to "Samvidhan Sadan".
Election Commission: Headquarters named "Nirvachan Sadan".
Awards Sansad Ratna Award 2026: Instituted in 2010 based on the suggestion of Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam. Given by Chennai-based NGO Prime Point Foundation to top-performing MPs. This year, 12 MPs received the award.
Defense & Space Shenzhou-23 Mission: Launched by China. Carried 3 astronauts to the Tiangong Space Station.
Agni-1 Missile: Short-Range Ballistic Missile (SRBM). Tested by DRDO. Range: 700 to 900 km. It is a single-stage solid-fuel missile.
Important Days May 25: World Football Day.

Agencies, Acts & Schemes

  • PM AJAY (Anusuchit Jaati Abhyuday Yojana): Launched in 2021-22 under the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment. It merges 3 schemes: Adarsh Gram Yojana, Babu Jagjivan Ram Chhatrawas Yojana, and Special Central Assistance to SC Sub-Plan. Recently, a dedicated mobile app was launched for this scheme.
  • Bhavya Scheme: Operates under the Ministry of Commerce and Industry. Aims to set up 100 plug-and-play world-class industrial parks in India.

⬡ Tamil Nadu Connect

  • Cooperative Crop Loan Waiver: TN Govt announced a 100% waiver on crop loans up to ₹50,000 for Marginal Farmers (owning up to 1 hectare / 2.5 acres). Small farmers (2.5 to 5 acres) are excluded from the 100% waiver, sparking debate regarding election manifestos.
  • Bureaucratic Appointments: Arun IPS (former Chennai Commissioner) was recently appointed as the Director of the Directorate of Vigilance and Anti-Corruption (DVAC).
03

The Mains Analytical Breakdown

Dimensional Analysis

Social Impact
  • The integration of multiple sub-schemes under PM AJAY stream-lines the socio-economic development and targeted financial upliftment of marginalized Scheduled Caste communities, enhancing operational efficiency.
Economic Impact
  • The Bhavya Scheme envisions 100 plug-and-play industrial parks, dramatically reducing setup times for manufacturers, which stimulates FDI and bolsters the "Make in India" initiative.
Political Impact
  • Recognitions like the Sansad Ratna Awards act as non-partisan motivators, encouraging robust legislative debate, attendance, and policy formulation among elected Members of Parliament.
Internal Security Impact
  • The rise of unauthorized digital/physical mobilizations (e.g., the mentioned "Cockroach Janata Party") poses insidious threats to domestic law and order, necessitating proactive regulation without infringing on democratic rights.
Pros & Cons Analysis

Advantages

  • Agriculture Relief: The TN crop loan waiver immediately alleviates the debt burden of marginal farmers, preventing distress sales and supporting foundational agrarian economies.
  • Strategic Defense: Successful testing of the Agni-1 (700-900 km SRBM) continues to secure India's regional deterrence capability.

Challenges

  • Policy Exclusion: Limiting the 100% crop loan waiver strictly to marginal farmers (under 2.5 acres) alienates small farmers (up to 5 acres), leading to trust deficits against political manifestos and causing agrarian discontent.
  • Fiscal Strain: Blanket loan waivers consistently place severe fiscal strain on State Exchequers and cooperative banking structures.

Relevant Committee Reports & Frameworks

  • Digital Welfare Frameworks: The launch of the PM AJAY mobile app aligns with digital governance mandates to reduce leakages in beneficiary funds (DBT) meant for specific community sub-plans.
