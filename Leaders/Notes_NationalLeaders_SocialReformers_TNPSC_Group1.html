💎 GEM Who first called him "Periyar"? — NOT Neelambigai Ammaiyar. It was Chidambaram Kudiyarasu (சிதம்பரம் குடியரசு) who called him Periyar informally before the official 1938 conferral. TNPSC has tested this distinction!
🎓 Education & Early Life
  • Had no formal higher education — self-educated through extensive reading and travel
  • Family background: Father Venkatappa Naicker ran a profitable commodity trading (Mandi) business — family was well-settled
  • 1898 (Age 19): Married Nagammai (age 13), his first cousin (aunt's daughter), despite family opposition
  • 1902 (Age 22–23): First inter-caste dining event (Samapanthi Bhojanam) + first inter-caste marriage ceremony he presided over (Velalar girl + Naidu boy)
  • 1904–1908: Renounced worldly life (துறவு கோலம்); traveled to Vijayawada, Kashi (Varanasi), Kolkata — first turning point
  • Kashi experience: At Kashi, he saw caste discrimination even in religious contexts. Combined with Bharati and other Tamil figures who visited Kashi and returned transformed, this period shaped his rationalism
  • 1905–1908: Observed plague(புபோனிக் பிளேக்) epidemic relief work — single-handedly carried plague-affected corpses when even relatives refused
💎 GEM Periyar's Kashi journey parallels Bharati's Kashi journey — both Tamil luminaries visited Kashi and came back transformed. Bharati returned with the iconic headband (முண்டாசு) and twirled mustache. Periyar returned with rationalist fire. Both stories show Kashi as a turning point for Tamil reformers.
⚡ Major Contributions
CRITICAL Periyar's anti-God stance arose from a specific logical argument: "If seeing me pollutes the deity, then that deity does not exist" — his atheism was born from the injustice of untouchables being barred from temples. His God-rejection was essentially an anti-caste argument first.

⚡ Self-Respect Movement — Deep Dive
CRITICAL The Self-Respect Movement (சுயமரியாதை இயக்கம்) was started in 1925. Its official newspaper was Kudiyarasu, started May 2, 1925. The movement aimed to end Brahmin dominance and uplift backward + depressed classes. It was NOT a political movement but a social reform movement.

Three Key Conferences (Mnemonic: S-E-V)

2nd1930Erode (ஈரோடு)Jayakar (from Pune)Demanded no caste suffixes in 1931 Census; Erode Plan framework

TIP Mnemonic for 3 Conference Cities: "S-E-V" → "SHAVE" (Periyar never shaved his beard!)
S = Chengalpattu · E = Erode · V = Virudhunagar
Years: 1929 → 1930 → 1931 (consecutive years)

Goals of Self-Respect Movement

  • Uplift backward and depressed classes; assert their self-dignity
  • Oppose Brahmin dominance in politics, religion, and society
  • Reform Dravidian/Tamil culture by returning to ancient Tamil values (before Aryan influence)
  • Eliminate superstitions and blind beliefs (மூடநம்பிக்கை ஒழிப்பு)
  • Champion women's education, widow remarriage, inter-caste marriage, end child marriage
  • Abolish Devadasi system and female infanticide (Kalli paal — killing girl infants with poisonous milk)
  • Spread rational thinking (பகுத்தறிவு)
PYQ TNPSC 2018 (Group 2): "The first Self-Respect Marriage in Tamil Nadu was conducted in which place?" → Answer: Shuklanatham(Aruppukkottai)(அருப்புக்கோட்டை), 1928 (near Madurai). Periyar presided over it himself.

⚡ Vaikkam Satyagraha (1924) — Critical Detail
Location
Vaikkam, Travancore State (now Kerala)
Objective
Allow Harijans (untouchables) to walk on roads near Vaikkam temple
Who Started It
Kerala's K. Kelappan (Vaikkam Harijan Sabha) + George Joseph (Madurai)
Who Joined
George Joseph contacted Periyar; Periyar joined with Rajaji and Thiru.Vi.Ka.
Imprisoned
Periyar jailed twice by Travancore State
Outcome
VICTORY! Travancore state removed the restriction. All people could use temple approach roads.
TRAP The Vaikkam Satyagraha was about road access near a temple (not entering the temple itself). The temple entry agitation for Travancore came later. Don't confuse these two separate events.
PYQ TNPSC Group 1 (2016): "The Criminal Tribes Act — who was the primary defender in Tamil Nadu?" → George Joseph (not Muthuramalingam Devar). George Joseph was from Madurai. Muthuramalingam Devar's contribution also important but George Joseph was the PRIMARY one per PYQ.
💬 Famous Quotes & Statements
"If seeing me pollutes the deity, then that deity does not exist." — Periyar's logical argument for atheism, arising from untouchables being barred from temples
"Tamil Nadu for Tamils (தமிழ்நாடு தமிழருக்கே)" — Proclaimed at Triplicane beach march, 1938, during anti-Hindi agitation

🌿 Tamil Nadu Connection & Impact
  • Erode, Tamil Nadu: birthplace; municipality chairman (1919); Self-Respect Movement HQ
  • Led Tamil Nadu's most consequential social reform movement — transformed caste surnames, temple access, marriage practices
  • Anti-Hindi agitation (1937–38): fought Rajaji's 125-school Hindi imposition; thousands jailed; two died at Triplicane protests
  • Dravidian political ideology foundation: his work laid the ground for Justice Party → DK → DMK → AIADMK chain
  • Tamil Nadu's Social Justice Day (சமூக நீதி நாள்) = September 17 in his honor
  • His second marriage prompted Anna's split → September 17, 1949 → DMK founded (on Periyar's birthday!)
  • Self-Respect Marriage Act (1968) by CM Anna fulfilling Periyar's lifelong dream
  • Periyar Thidal, Chennai: his resting place and permanent memorial

  • 1920: Brought Periyar into Congress — crucial link between the two leaders

Died

Title
Mahatma · Bapu · Father of the Nation
⚡ Key Connections to Tamil Nadu & Periyar
  • Periyar was initially a Gandhi follower (1920–1927) — wore khadi, participated in Non-Cooperation
  • 1927: Periyar permanently broke with Gandhi — "I will no longer call Gandhi 'Mahatma'"
  • Gandhi called untouchables "Harijans" (children of God/Hari) — Periyar and Ambedkar both criticized this
  • Gandhi said about Nagammai and Kannamma: "Even if I wanted to stop the Non-Cooperation Movement in Madras, those two women would not allow it"
  • Rajaji (Rajagopalachari) was Gandhi's close associate and son-in-law
  • Gandhi's support for Charkha led Periyar to cut 500 coconut trees in 1921
TRAP Gandhi gave the term "Harijan" to Scheduled Castes = children of God (Hari = Vishnu). However, many SC leaders including Ambedkar and Periyar rejected this term as demeaning/patronizing. Don't assume "Harijan" was universally accepted.

2026 TNPSC Group 1 — High-Probability Questions

Based on pattern analysis of 2010–2024 papers. These 5 questions have the highest probability of appearing in 2026 Group 1.

Q1. Who first used the term "Periyar" for E.V. Ramasamy before the official conferral in 1938?
Chidambaram Kudiyarasu (சிதம்பரம் குடியரசு)
⚡ Why likely: This fact appears in Ka. Venkatesan's reference book but NOT in standard textbooks. Group 1 frequently tests such extra-textbook facts. Previously tested once; cycle suggests retesting.
Q2. In which city did Periyar cut 500 trees for Gandhi's non-cooperation movement, and what type of trees were they?
Erode — 500 COCONUT trees (thenai maram), not palmyra (panai maram)
⚡ Why likely: Classic TRAP question. Many candidates answer "palmyra" — the correct answer is coconut. Examiners test precise botanical knowledge. Toddy is extracted from both, but Periyar's grove had COCONUT trees.
Q3. Match the Self-Respect Movement conferences: Chengalpattu / Erode / Virudhunagar → 1929 / 1930 / 1931 with their respective presidents
Chengalpattu 1929 → WPA Sountharapandian | Erode 1930 → Jayakar (from Pune) | Virudhunagar 1931 → R.K. Shanmugam Chettyar
⚡ Why likely: Conference–city–year–president matching is a classic Group 1 format. Previously asked in Group 2; Group 1 will add the president names for difficulty.
Q4. Periyar's book "Karppa Aatchi" deals with which subject? In which year was it published?
Family planning / Birth control (குடும்ப கட்டுப்பாடு) — 1930
⚡ Why likely: Asked in Group 2/4 multiple times. Now being elevated to Group 1 with additional context questions about its content or impact.
Q5. The Dravidar Kazhagam was founded at which city? Who suggested the name "Dravidar Kazhagam" and in which year?
Founded: Salem, 1944. Name suggested by: Arignar Anna in 1942 (2 years before DK was actually formed)
⚡ Why likely: The 2-year gap between suggestion (1942 by Anna) and actual formation (1944 by Periyar) is a precision fact that Group 1 loves. Combined question format likely in 2026.
