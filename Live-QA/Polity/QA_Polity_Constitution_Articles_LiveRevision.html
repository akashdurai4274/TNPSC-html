🏛️ Union Legislature / Rajya Sabha Articles

Medium Factual / Conceptual

Q1. Which Article describes the power of the Deputy Chairman or other person to perform the duties of the office of, or to act as, Chairman of Rajya Sabha?

துணைத்தலைவர் அல்லது மற்ற நபரின் நாடாளுமன்ற மாநிலங்களவை அலுவலக பணியை செய்ய தலைவராக செயல்படுவதற்கான அதிகாரத்தை எந்த சரத்து விவரிக்கிறது?

Q1
▼
✅ CORRECT ANSWER

Live Chat Mistakes: Article 92 → Article 91

[RULE LINK] Constitutional Mechanism

Under Article 91, when the office of the Chairman of the Council of States (Rajya Sabha) is vacant, or during any period when the Vice-President is acting as or discharging the functions of President, the duties of the office shall be performed by the Deputy Chairman.

[TNPSC / SSC TRAP] Article 92 Overlap

Article 92 explicitly states that the Chairman or Deputy Chairman shall not preside while a resolution for their removal from office is under consideration. Students frequently mix this up with Article 91's officiating power.

[💎 LIVE GEM] Voting Limitations under Removal Resolution

Live Discussion Point: Article 92 Sub-clause (2) clarifies that when a removal resolution is active against the Chairman (Vice President), they have the right to speak in the proceedings but no right to vote at all on the resolution, breaking the standard tie-breaker rule.

⚖️ Union Judiciary & Appellate Jurisdiction

Hard Conceptual

Q2. Which Article of the Constitution deals with the enlargement of the jurisdiction of the Supreme Court?

உச்ச நீதிமன்றத்தின் அதிகார வரம்பை விரிவுபடுத்துவது பற்றி எந்த சரத்து விவரிக்கிறது?

Q2
▼
✅ CORRECT ANSWER

Article 138 (Options checked: 134, 135, 137, 138)

🏆 PYQ ALERT (TNPSC Gr 1 / SSC CGL)

The core provisions of Supreme Court jurisdictions (Articles 131 - 143) are standard recurring match-the-following items in state services exams over the past decade.

[💎 LIVE GEM] Article 135 Explained via Recent Events

Faculty connected Article 135 (Jurisdiction and powers of the Federal Court under existing law to be exercisable by the Supreme Court) to an appellate jurisdiction issue arising out of the recent Vilathikulam double death penalty case where mercy petitions and sequential appeals highlight how structural appellate power functions.

[CRITICAL] Key Overlaps

Article 137: Review of judgments or orders by the Supreme Court (Power of Judicial Review of its own rulings).

👔 State Executive & Legislative Protections

Medium Conceptual / Mathematical Shortcut

Q3. Which Article details the duties of the Chief Minister regarding the furnishing of information to the Governor?

ஆளுனருக்கு மாநில நிர்வாக தகவல் அளிப்பது போன்ற முதலமைச்சரின் கடமைகளை எந்த சரத்து விவரிக்கிறது?

Q3
▼
✅ CORRECT ANSWER

Article 167

[TIP] The "+89" Magical Mnemonic Rule

To easily map Central Government structural articles to equivalent State Government articles, use the 89 addition rule:

Article 78 (Duties of PM to President) + 89 = Article 167 (Duties of CM to Governor).

[💎 LIVE GEM] Constitutional Bridge Concept

The Chief Minister functions as the communication bridge between the Council of Ministers and the Governor at the state level, identical to how the Prime Minister links the Cabinet with the President at the Union level.

Medium Factual / Regional History

Q4. Which Article deals with the abolition or creation of Legislative Councils (State Legislative Council - MLC) in States?

மாநிலங்களில் சட்ட மேலவையை உருவாக்குதல் அல்லது நீக்குதல் பற்றி எந்த சரத்து விவரிக்கிறது?

Q4
▼
✅ CORRECT ANSWER

Article 169

🏆 PYQ ALERT - TAMIL NADU FACT HISTORY

Important Milestone: Tamil Nadu possessed a bicameral legislature until the State Legislative Council was abolished under Chief Minister M.G. Ramachandran via a resolution executed on November 1, 1986 using the Article 169 framework.

Hard Factual / Separation of Powers

Q5. Which Article states that courts should not inquire into the proceedings of the State Legislature?

சட்டமன்றத்தின் செயல்பாடுகளை நீதிமன்றங்கள் விசாரிக்கக் கூடாது என்று கூறும் சரத்து எது?

Q5
▼
✅ CORRECT ANSWER

Article 212

[TIP] Central Mapping Analogy

Apply the subtraction configuration conceptually: Article 122 bars courts from inquiring into Parliamentary proceedings. Swapping and manipulating structural patterns brings you directly to Article 212 for the state legislatures.

Easy Factual

Q6. Which Article establishes that there shall be a High Court for each State?

ஒவ்வொரு மாநிலத்திற்கும் ஒரு உயர் நீதிமன்றம் இருக்க வேண்டும் என்று கூறும் சரத்து எது?

Q6
▼
✅ CORRECT ANSWER

Article 214

[💎 LIVE GEM] Common High Court Provisions

Article 231 authorizes the establishment of a Common High Court for two or more states or Union Territories. This modification was enabled by the 7th Constitutional Amendment Act of 1956. India currently operates 6 common High Courts.

🏡 Local Self-Government & Finance Provisions

Hard Trick / Structural

Q7. Which specific Article explains the power to impose taxes by, and funds of, the Municipalities?

நகராட்சிகள் மூலம் வரிகளை விதிக்கும் அதிகாரம் மற்றும் அதன் நிதி பற்றி எந்த சரத்து கூறுகிறது?

Q7
▼
✅ CORRECT ANSWER

Article 243X

[CRITICAL] Capital vs Small Alphabet Case Notation

Capital letter suffixes (e.g., 243A to 243ZG) denote post-amendment additions enacted via the 73rd & 74th Amendments (1992). Lowercase suffixes represent original text configurations explicitly structural to the 1950 constitution.

[SSC TRAP] Panchayat vs Municipality Taxation Overlap

Panchayat Tax Power: Article 243H

Municipality Tax Power: Article 243X

Medium Factual / Accounting

Q8. Which Article dictates the Consolidated Funds and Public Accounts of India and of the States?

இந்தியா மற்றும் மாநிலங்களின் ஒருங்கிணைந்த நிதிகள் மற்றும் பொதுக்கணக்குகள் பற்றி விவரிக்கும் சரத்து எது?

Q8
▼
✅ CORRECT ANSWER

Article 266

[💎 LIVE GEM] Public Accounts Simplified via Real Life Examples

Faculty explained that while tax revenues deposit into the Consolidated Fund (Article 266), small scale public savings like the Public Provident Fund (PPF) managed via Post Office schemes or Government employee provident funds sit inside the Public Account of India because the state acts merely as a temporary custodian.

சரக்கு மற்றும் சேவை வரி கவுன்சில் (GST Council) பற்றி எந்த சரத்து விவரிக்கிறது?

🏆 PYQ ALERT - AMENDMENT MATCHING

[CRITICAL] Clarifying the Civil Services Range Overlap

Article 311: Deals explicitly with dismissal, removal, or reduction in rank of civil servants. It does not dictate the institutional creation of the commissions themselves.

[💎 LIVE GEM] Current Institutional Leaders (2026 Check)

The current operational leader structure includes the UPSC Chairperson at the national level and structural transitions within the State Public Service Commission (TNPSC) framework.

வயது வந்தோர் வாக்குரிமையின் அடிப்படையில் லோக்சபா மற்றும் மாநில சட்டமன்றங்களுக்கான தேர்தல்கள் நடத்தப்பட வேண்டும் என்று கூறும் சரத்து எது?

🏆 PYQ ALERT - AGE AMENDMENT LEVEL

Voting age eligibility was lowered from 21 years down to 18 years via the landmark 61st Constitutional Amendment Act, 1989.

Medium Property Rights History

Q12. Which constitutional article ensures that persons shall not be deprived of property save by authority of law?

சட்டப்பூர்வ அதிகாரத்தால் தவிர தனிநபர்களின் சொத்துக்களை பறிக்கக் கூடாது என்று கூறும் சரத்து எது?

Q12
▼
✅ CORRECT ANSWER

Article 300A

[RULE LINK] Structural Relocation of Fundamental Rights

Originally, the Right to Property stood as a fully operational Fundamental Right under Article 19(1)(f) and Article 31. The 44th Constitutional Amendment Act of 1978 stripped this protection away, migrating it over to Part XII as a regular Legal/Constitutional Right under Article 300A.

[💎 LIVE GEM] The Catalyst Case: Kesavananda Bharati

The intense structural struggle between individual religious property rights (monastery land management across Kerala under HH Kesavananda Bharati) and state property land ceiling laws served as the prime catalyst that ultimately birthed the Basic Structure Doctrine of 1973.

Hard Language Provisions / Factual

Q13. Which Article provides for the Commission and Committee of Parliament on Official Language?

அலுவல் மொழி பற்றிய நாடாளுமன்றத்தின் ஆணையம் மற்றும் குழு பற்றி விவரிக்கும் சரத்து எது?

Q13
▼
✅ CORRECT ANSWER

Live Chat Mistakes: Article 343 → Article 344

[TNPSC TRAP] Official Language vs Institutional Commission

Article 343: Declares the core Official Language of the Union (Hindi in Devanagari script).

Article 344: Directs the operational setup of Parliamentary commissions and evaluation committees tracking language implementation progress.

[💎 LIVE GEM] Debunking National Language Myths

CRITICAL FACT: India has NO National Language. The Indian state operates explicitly with designated official operational languages. Eighth Schedule recognizes 22 distinct regional languages, expanding significantly from the initial base of 14 languages.

தேசிய அவசரநிலை பிரகடனத்தின் போது, சரத்து 19-ல் உள்ள சுதந்திர உரிமைகள் தானாகவே தற்காலிகமாக நீக்கப்படுவதை விவரிக்கும் சரத்து எது?

HW
▼
✅ COMPLETE EXPANDED SOLUTION

Article 358

[CRITICAL MASTER COMPARISON] Article 358 vs Article 359
Feature Criterion Article 358 Article 359
Scope Suspends Fundamental Rights under Article 19 only. Suspends enforcement of other specified Fundamental Rights.
Automation Automatic suspension the moment emergency is declared. Requires a separate specific Presidential Order.
Absolute Exception Article 19 rights cannot be restored until emergency ends. Articles 20 and 21 can NEVER be suspended.

📋 10-POINT HIGH-YIELD REVISION SUMMARY

  • Article 91 permits the Deputy Chairman to assume executive control when the Rajya Sabha Chairman's post falls vacant.
  • Article 92 strips the presiding officer of their standard casting/tie-breaking vote while a removal resolution targets them.
  • Article 138 delegates the enlargement power of Supreme Court jurisdictional reach directly to Parliament.
  • Article 167 frames the non-negotiable statutory duty of the Chief Minister to report administration facts to the Governor.
  • The mathematical 89 Addition Rule allows fast conversion from federal machinery provisions to regional state articles.
  • Article 169 governs structural creation/abolition of legislative councils, famously used in Tamil Nadu on November 1, 1986.
  • Local government tax powers partition distinctly: 243H protects Panchayats, while 243X guides Municipalities.
  • The GST Council (Article 279A) stands as a landmark cooperative federal entity created under the 101st Amendment.
  • Article 300A reclassified property ownership from a fundamental protection into a statutory constitutional legal right.
  • India functions completely with Official Languages (Article 343/344) and holds zero concept of a singular national language.
