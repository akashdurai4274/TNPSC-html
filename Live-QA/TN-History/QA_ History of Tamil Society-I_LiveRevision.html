📋 Session Overview & Analysis

This session deals comprehensively with TNPSC Unit 8 / Unit 6 criteria covering the Socio-Political History of Tamil Nadu. It forms a high-weightage block responsible for up to 40 questions in Group 1 exams and around 20 questions in Group 2/2A & 4. The session addresses critical focus areas including justice administration, subaltern movements, anti-colonial resistance of Palayakkarars, and Sangam literature values.

Sub-Topic 1: Leaders, Administrative Statements & Philosophies

Q1 · [MEDIUM] · [Conceptual]
Medium Conceptual
Who stated the administrative philosophy: "One should not accept any post to which one cannot execute full justice" (ஒருவர் முழு நீதியை செய்ய முடியாத எந்த பதவியையும் ஏற்க கூடாது)?
✅ ANSWER:

B.R. Ambedkar / Mahatma Gandhi / Jawaharlal Nehru → K. Kamaraj (கர்மவீரர் காமராஜர்)

🪤 TNPSC TRAP NOTE: Eliminate via Syllabus Context

Live students immediately guessed B.R. Ambedkar due to the presence of the word "Justice". However, keep track of your subject framework. Since this topic is entirely focused on Tamil Nadu's Socio-Political history, evaluate state leaders first. Kamaraj made this landmark statement regarding personal and administrative accountability.

💎 LIVE GEM: Cross-Reference to Indian Polity Textbook

The teacher highlighted another famous statement by K. Kamaraj found in standard School Polity books: "Laws are only for the people; people are not for the laws" (சட்டம் என்பது மக்களுக்கானது; மக்கள் சட்டவழிகளுக்கானவர்கள் அல்ல). Note this down for statements-based questions.

Q2 · [MEDIUM] · [Factual]
Medium Factual
Which District Collector submitted a groundbreaking historic report in 1892 regarding the socio-economic deprivations of scheduled communities, eventually leading to the assignment of Panchami Lands?
✅ ANSWER:

James Tremenheere (ஜேம்ஸ் ட்ரெமென்ஹீர்), the then District Collector of Chingleput (செங்கல்பட்டு).

🏆 PYQ ALERT: TNPSC Group 2 & 4 Core Repeat

This exact historical event forms the historical core background of subaltern lands rights in Tamil Nadu. Tremenheere's report of 1892 directly initiated the institutional allocation of Panchami Lands to Depressed Classes to mitigate institutional landlessness.

Sub-Topic 2: Justice Party Era & Non-Brahmin Consolidation

Q3 · [EASY] · [Factual]
Easy Factual
Who was selected as the first legislative council member to directly represent the depressed classes in the Madras Legislative Council?
✅ ANSWER:

M.C. Rajah (மயிலை சின்னத்தம்பி ராஜா - MC ராஜா)

💎 LIVE GEM: Legislative Leadership Distinction

M.C. Rajah was not only the first from depressed classes to enter the Madras Legislative Council, but he also served as the Deputy Leader of the house within the framework of council proceedings alongside the Justice Party layout.

Q4 · [HARD] · [Trick/Trap]
Hard Trick/Trap
Identify the incorrect statement(s) from the given pairs:
1) Suryanarayana Sastri is considered the Father of Tamil Linguistic Purism.
2) Moovalur Ramamirtham Ammaiyar was the first female legislator in India.
✅ ANSWER:

Both statements 1 and 2 are INCORRECT (இரண்டுமே தவறானவை)

⚠️ CRITICAL REVISION BREAKDOWN: [CORRECTION NOTE]

1. The Father of Tamil Linguistic Purism (தனித்தமிழ் இயக்கத்தின் தந்தை) is Maraimalai Adigal (மறைமலை அடிகள்), who changed his Sanskritized name Vedachalam into pure Tamil. Suryanarayana Sastri (Parithimar Kalaignar) was a pioneer who declared Tamil as a Classical Language.
2. The first female legislator in India was Dr. Muthulakshmi Reddy (டாக்டர் முத்துலட்சுமி ரெட்டி) in 1926, hailing from Pudukkottai region, not Moovalur Ramamirtham Ammaiyar.

Q5 · [MEDIUM] · [Conceptual]
Medium Conceptual
Whose prime structural objective was to work systematically through a constitutional framework for the upliftment of non-Brahmins across education and employment?
✅ ANSWER:

The Justice Party (நீதிக்கட்சி - South Indian Liberal Federation)

🪤 TNPSC TRAP: Differentiating Ideological Offshoots

Do not confuse the Self-Respect Movement (சுயமரியாதை இயக்கம்) or Dravidar Kazhagam (திராவிடர் கழகம்) with the Justice Party here. The Self-Respect Movement and DK explicitly chose not to contest elections or form constitutional governments. Whenever the prompt implies working specifically "through constitutional governance and legislative channels", choose Justice Party.

Q6 · [MEDIUM] · [Factual]
Medium Factual
In which year was the Madras Non-Brahmin Association (சென்னை பிராமணர் அல்லாதார் சங்கம்) originally initiated?
✅ ANSWER:

1909

💎 GEM: Evolutionary Tree of the Dravidian Movement

The teacher mapped out the structural lineage of the movement for chronological order questions:
1909: Madras Non-Brahmin Association (formed by lawyers P. Subramanyam and M. Purushothaman Naidu)
↓
1912: Madras United League (later renamed Madras Dravidian Association)
↓
1916: South Indian Liberal Federation (SILF / Justice Party)
↓
1944: Renamed as Dravidar Kazhagam (DK) at the Salem Conference.

Q7 · [HARD] · [Factual]
Hard Factual
Who compiled statistical data for the Royal Commission in 1913, proving that Brahmins (forming only 3% of the population) commanded over 90%+ of public employment positions?
✅ ANSWER:

Alexander Gordon Cardew (அலெக்சாண்டர் கார்டன் கார்டியூ)

🏆 PYQ ALERT: Statistical Subsets of Reservation History

Cardew's detailed evidence submitted to the Public Services Commission provided the primary evidence that led to the introduction of communal government orders (Communal G.O.s) providing reservations in Madras Presidency.

Sub-Topic 3: Tamil Literature, Epics & Cultural Landscapes

Q8 · [MEDIUM] · [Factual]
Medium Factual
Match the following epic husbands with their respective spouses based on standard Tamil literary contexts:
1) Pandu, 2) Rama, 3) Ravana, 4) Dhritarashtra.
✅ ANSWER:

• Pandu (பாண்டு) — Kunti (குந்தி)
• Rama (ராமன்) — Sita (சீதை)
• Ravana (ராவணன்) — Mandodari (மண்டோதரி)
• Dhritarashtra (திருதராஷ்டிரன்) — Gandhari (காந்தாரி)

Q9 · [HARD] · [Conceptual]
Hard Conceptual
"Many can sleep, but he sleeps not; he watches the world with high moral standard..." (பலர் துஞ்சவும் தான் துஞ்சான் உலகு காக்கும் உயர் கொள்கை கேட்டான்) - Which text contains these lines?
✅ ANSWER:

Purananuru (புறநானூறு) / Narrinai (நற்றினை) → Akananuru (அகநானூறு)

🪤 TRAP NOTE: The Worldly Protection Trap

Most students quickly choose Purananuru because the verse talks about statecraft, kingly duty, and protecting the world. However, this specific line belongs to the Akananuru collection. Memorize this to avoid losing marks on literary quotes.

Sub-Topic 4: Palayakkarar Rebellion & Anti-Colonial Resistance

Q10 · [MEDIUM] · [Factual]
Medium Factual
On what exact calendar date did Major Bannerman issue his final ultimatum ordering Veerapandiya Kattabomman to surrender at Palayamkottai?
✅ ANSWER:

September 1, 1799 (செப்டம்பர் 1, 1799)

💎 LIVE GEM: Chronology of the Fall of Panchalankurichi

The teacher detailed the exact timelines often tested in Group 1/2 exams:
• Sept 1, 1799: Ultimatum delivered via Ramalinganar.
• Sept 5, 1799: British army attacks Panchalankurichi fort.
• Sept 13, 1799: Kattabomman's minister Sivasubramanian Pillai executed at Nagalapuram.
• Oct 16, 1799: Kattabomman tried before an assembly of Palayakkarars.
• Oct 17, 1799: Kattabomman hanged from a tamarind tree at Kayathar.

📌 10-Point Core Revision Summary

  • Kamaraj Philosophy: Advocated that no individual should hold an administrative seat if they cannot deliver complete, fair justice.
  • Panchami Land Genesis: Formulated from Collector James Tremenheere's 1892 investigative report in Chingleput.
  • M.C. Rajah Eminence: Pioneer leader of the depressed classes who also served as Madras Legislative Council's Deputy Leader.
  • Linguistic Purism: Spearheaded primarily by Maraimalai Adigal, who systematically eliminated Sanskrit structures from Tamil.
  • Dravidian Roots: The movement began with the Madras Non-Brahmin Association in 1909, before evolving into the Justice Party.
  • Cardew Commission Data: Statistically demonstrated that 3% of the population held structural dominance over public services.
  • Epic Pairings: Kunti, Sita, Mandodari, and Gandhari are matched as spouses to Pandu, Rama, Ravana, and Dhritarashtra respectively.
  • Akananuru Quote: Contains the lines stating how an idealistic leader stays awake protecting society while others sleep.
  • Anti-Colonial Timelines: Major Bannerman deployed his ultimatum to Kattabomman on September 1, 1799.
  • Sivasubramanian Execution: Hanged publicly by British forces at Nagalapuram on September 13, 1799.
