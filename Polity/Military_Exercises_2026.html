[CRITICAL] STRATEGIC IMPORTANCE

Military exercises are heavily targeted in upcoming examination cycles. Pay special attention to newly introduced geographic nodes like Kampong Speu, newly mapped multi-lateral engagements (MILAN 2026 involving 72 nations), and standard bi-laterals undergoing structural enhancements.

[TRAP] NOMENCLATURE & REPETITION

Do not confuse recurring annual exercises like Suryakiran or Dharma Guardian across years. Note the specific locations which alternate between India and host nations (e.g., Suryakiran 2026 in Saljhandi, Nepal vs Suryakiran 2025 in Pithoragarh, India).
