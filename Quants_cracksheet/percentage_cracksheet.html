The six traps that cause 80% of mistakes

  • Picking the wrong base in "A is what % more/less than B" (Pattern 5).
  • Subtracting % directly instead of dividing by the correct factor in reverse-% problems (Pattern 3).
  • Confusing "percentage points" with "percent" (Pattern 15).
  • Missing the "BY" vs "TO" distinction in wording (Pattern 17).
  • Forgetting to recompute valid votes before applying election % logic (Pattern 8).
  • Applying sequential expenditure % to the original amount instead of the remaining amount (Pattern 7).
