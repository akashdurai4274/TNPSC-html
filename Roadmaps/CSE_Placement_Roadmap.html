Zero to Hero Guide

Complete Placement Preparation Strategy

Tailored for final-year Computer Science engineering students starting from the basics. Focus on high-yield DSA patterns, core CS concepts, modern AI-integrated full-stack projects, and strategic job applications.

6 Months Timeline
150+ DSA Problems
3 Core Subjects
2 Full-Stack Projects
Weeks 1 - 3
Phase 01

Core Foundation

Master one primary programming language and set up your development environment.

  • Choose C++, Java, or Python (Stick to ONE).
  • Pointers, Memory, Loops, & Exception Handling.
  • OOPs: Encapsulation, Abstraction, Polymorphism.
  • Git/GitHub basics (Commit, Push, PRs).
Goal: Write bug-free basic code without looking up syntax.
Months 1 - 3
Phase 02

DSA & Problem Solving

Crack Online Assessments (OAs) by solving 150+ structured pattern problems.

  • Arrays, Strings, Two Pointers, Sliding Window.
  • Recursion, Backtracking, Stacks & Queues.
  • Trees (BFS/DFS), BST, & Graphs.
  • Dynamic Programming & Greedy Patterns.
Target: 2 problems/day on LeetCode/GeeksforGeeks.
Months 3 - 4
Phase 03

CS Fundamentals

Clear technical interviews by mastering core computer science subjects.

  • DBMS: SQL Queries, Joins, Indexing, ACID.
  • OS: Threads, Deadlocks, Virtual Memory.
  • CN: OSI Model, TCP/IP, HTTP/S, DNS.
  • Aptitude: Quantitative & Logical Drills.
Goal: Explain OS/DBMS concepts orally with clarity.
Months 4 - 5
Phase 04

Modern Web & AI Stack

Build industry-grade projects that set your resume apart from the competition.

  • MERN Stack (React, Node.js, Express, Mongo) or Python/FastAPI.
  • REST APIs & Auth (JWT / OAuth2).
  • Integrate LLM APIs / AI Workflows (RAG).
  • Deployment on Vercel / Render / AWS.
Goal: 2 deployed projects with GitHub source code.
Months 5 - 6
Phase 05

Resume, Mocks & Applications

Execute high-conversion job search strategies on-campus and off-campus.

  • Single-page LaTeX Resume (Jake's Resume template).
  • Mock Technical & HR Interviews on Pramp.
  • Targeted LinkedIn Outreach & Referral Messaging.
  • Company-specific PYQ practice (Service & Product).
Goal: Secure interview calls & land offers. Final Goal
